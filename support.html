Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add friends?
From the Add Friends screen, enter a friend’s phone number to send a request. They’ll receive a notification to accept or decline.
How does location sharing work?
Location sharing is opt-in and only visible to accepted friends. You can enable/disable it anytime in Profile → Privacy. If disabled, your location is not shared.
What are auto statuses?
YAPP can set lightweight statuses based on device signals (e.g., Driving, On Call, Do Not Disturb, Silent). You can turn auto statuses off in Settings → Status.
How do I change my status?
Tap your profile/status and choose a custom status. Auto statuses may temporarily override while active (e.g., during a phone call).
Why can’t I see my friends in the widget?
Ensure you’ve added friends and they’re active. Open the app to refresh. On iOS, check Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services and allow YAPP access.
I’m not receiving notifications.
On iPhone: Settings → Notifications → YAPP, allow alerts/badges/sounds. Make sure Focus/Do Not Disturb isn’t blocking alerts. Then open YAPP once to re-register.
How do I delete my account?
In the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated data, subject to legal/anti-abuse retention.

Troubleshooting Basics

  • Open YAPP → log out and back in.
  • Check App Store for updates to YAPP.
  • Restart your iPhone.
  • For location issues: ensure Location Services are enabled for YAPP.
  • For notifications: confirm YAPP notifications are allowed and not blocked by Focus.
